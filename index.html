Today’s Financial Reality

Despite disciplined cost management and below‑average costs per discharge, many hospitals are under sustained financial pressure.

Operating margins, 2024

In 2024, 51% of Pennsylvania hospitals had margins below the commonly accepted 4% sustainability threshold, and 37% operated at a loss.

Methods and Sources

This dashboard summarizes key findings from the report “Time to Act: What The Future Holds For Pennsylvania’s Hospitals, Patients, and Communities” (January 2026). The analysis was prepared by Oliver Wyman and commissioned by The Hospital and Healthsystem Association of Pennsylvania (HAP).

  • Quantitative values (for example, margins, closures, reimbursement levels, job and wage impacts) are taken directly from the report text and its endnotes.
  • Scenario labels and 2030 net margins are based on the report’s modeling of Baseline, Baseline with H.R. 1, Rainy Day, and Sustainability Prevails paths.
  • The policy lever tool groups scenario drivers from Appendix A into four high‑level levers and provides illustrative mappings only. It does not calculate new projections.

For full detail, see the original report and appendix, including methodology, assumptions, and data sources.
